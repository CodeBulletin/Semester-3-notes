Reasoning with uncertinity

Probabilistic Reasoning

A knowledge representation where the concept of probability is applied to indicate uncertainty.

Need of probabilistic reasoning in AI:

  1. When there are unpredictable outcomes.
  2. When specifications or possibilities of predicates becomes too large to handle.
  3. When an unknown error occurs during an experiment. 

Probability: Probability can be defined as a chance that an uncertain event will occur. It is the numerical measure of the likelihood that an event will occur. The value of probability always remains between 0 and 1 that represent ideal uncertainties.
