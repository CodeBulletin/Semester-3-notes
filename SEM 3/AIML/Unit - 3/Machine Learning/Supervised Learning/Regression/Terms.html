Terms

Continuous variables: Continuous variables are variables that can take on any value within a certain range or interval. They can have decimal places and can be measured on a continuous scale. Examples of continuous variables include age, height, weight, temperature, and income.

Target Variables: In the context of supervised learning, the target variable is the variable that we want to predict or estimate based on the input features. It is also known as the dependent variable or the outcome variable. The target variable is typically a continuous numerical value in regression tasks or a categorical class label in classification tasks.

Dependent Variables: Dependent variables are variables that are influenced or affected by other variables. In the context of regression analysis, the dependent variable is the variable being predicted or explained by the independent variables. It is also referred to as the response variable or the outcome variable.

Outcome Variables: Outcome variables are the variables of interest in a study or analysis. They represent the results or outcomes that are being measured or observed. In the context of regression or predictive modeling, the outcome variable is the variable we want to predict or estimate.

Feature Variables: Feature variables, also known as independent variables or predictor variables, are the input variables used in a machine learning model to make predictions or classifications. They are the variables that provide information or characteristics about the data points or instances. Feature variables can be continuous or categorical.

Independent Variables: Independent variables, also known as predictor variables or explanatory variables, are variables that are used to explain or predict the values of the dependent or outcome variable. They are the variables that are manipulated or controlled in an experiment or analysis to observe their effect on the dependent variable.

M (Example): Let's consider an example of predicting house prices. In this case, the continuous variable "price" would be the target variable or dependent variable. The feature variables or independent variables could include factors such as the size of the house, the number of bedrooms, the location, and the age of the house. The goal would be to build a regression model that can predict the price of a house based on these features.
