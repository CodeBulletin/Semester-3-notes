DFS

DFS stands for Depth-First Search. It is a graph traversal algorithm that explores vertices or nodes of a graph by visiting the deepest vertices first and backtracking when necessary.

DFS follows the principle of visiting a node and recursively exploring all the unvisited neighbors of that node before backtracking. It traverses the graph in a depthward motion until it reaches a vertex that has no unvisited neighbors. It then backtracks to the previous vertex and explores the next unvisited neighbor.
