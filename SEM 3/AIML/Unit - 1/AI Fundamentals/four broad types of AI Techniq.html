four broad types of AI Techniques

There are four broad types of AI techniques or approaches, each with its own characteristics and applications:

Symbolic AI: Utilizes explicit rules and logic for problem-solving, encoding human knowledge into systems. Suited for structured data and clear-rule problems, like in expert systems.

Machine Learning (ML): A data-centric approach where algorithms learn from data to make predictions or decisions. It includes supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning, applied in image recognition, NLP, and predictive analytics.

Neural Networks and Deep Learning: Models simulating the human brain, with interconnected nodes. Deep Learning uses large, layered networks for complex tasks like image/speech recognition and autonomous driving. Examples include CNNs and RNNs.

Evolutionary Algorithms: Inspired by natural selection, these algorithms evolve solutions over generations, useful in optimization and design automation. Techniques include genetic algorithms and particle swarm optimization
