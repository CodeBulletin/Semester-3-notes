Types of AI

Based on the capability and functionality, there are two types of AI:

  • Type-I 
  • Type-II

Type 1 AI:

  1. Narrow AI (ANI): Specialized AI for specific tasks, like virtual assistants (Siri, Alexa) and recommendation systems (Netflix).
  2. General AI (AGI): Hypothetical AI with human-level intelligence across various domains. AGI is not yet a reality.
  3. Artificial Superintelligence (ASI): Advanced, theoretical AI surpassing human intelligence in every aspect. Currently speculative.

Type 2 AI:

  1. Reactive Machines: AI that operates on predefined rules for specific tasks, like chess programs. These systems don't learn or adapt.
  2. Limited Memory AI: AI capable of learning from past data to a limited extent, used in applications like self-driving cars.
  3. Theory of Mind AI: Hypothetical AI that could understand human emotions and intentions. Still under development and not fully realized.
  4. Self-aware AI: Conceptual AI with self-awareness and consciousness. Currently speculative and a subject of debate.
