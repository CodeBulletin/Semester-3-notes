AI

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the creation of machines that simulate human intelligence, capable of tasks like problem-solving, learning, and environment perception. It includes technologies like machine learning, natural language processing, and robotics, with applications in healthcare, finance, education, and more, aiming to enhance efficiency and decision-making.

AI systems are of two types:

  1. Narrow AI: These systems are task-specific, excelling in predefined activities but lacking general intelligence. Examples include virtual assistants and image recognition software.
  2. General AI: A theoretical concept of AI with human-level intelligence, capable of diverse tasks and learning from experiences. This type of AI is not yet realized.

AI's potential for industry revolution is significant, but it also presents ethical and safety challenges that need attention as the technology evolves.
