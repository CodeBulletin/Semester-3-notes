Symbolic AI

Symbolic AI, or rule-based AI, employs symbols, rules, and logic to represent knowledge and solve problems. Key features include:

  1. Symbolic Representation: Information is encoded using symbols (words, numbers, etc.) for task execution.
  2. Rule-Based Reasoning: Uses explicit rules and logic for decision-making and drawing conclusions.
  3. Knowledge Base: Contains facts, rules, and relationships to answer questions and solve problems.
  4. Inference Engine: Processes rules and symbols for logical deductions and conclusions.
  5. Declarative Knowledge: Describes facts and relationships, allowing the system to apply knowledge through rules.
  6. Transparency: The decision-making process is traceable and explainable via rules and symbols used.
