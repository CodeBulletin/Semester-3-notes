 The U.S. adult population by age is as follows (The World Almanac, 2009). The data are in millions of people.

total = 227.6

Assume that a person will be randomly chosen from this population. What is the probability the 

(i) person is 18 to 24 years old

\(P( 18 \le x \le 24) = \frac{29.8}{227.6} = 0.1309\)

(ii) person is 18 to 34 years old 

\(P(18 \le x \le 34) = \frac{29.8 + 40}{227.6} = 0.3066\)

(iii) person is 45 or older?

\(P(x \ge 45) =  \frac{43.9 + 32.7 + 37.8}{227.6} = 0.5026\)
