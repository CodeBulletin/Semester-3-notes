Examples

Nominal Scale:

  • Categories of car brands: Toyota, Ford, Honda, Chevrolet, etc.
  • Colors: Red, Blue, Green, Yellow, etc.
  • Animal classifications: Mammal, Reptile, Bird, Amphibian, etc.

Ordinal Scale:

  • Customer satisfaction ratings: Very Dissatisfied, Dissatisfied, Neutral, Satisfied, Very Satisfied.
  • Educational levels: Elementary, Middle School, High School, Bachelor's Degree, Master's Degree, PhD.
  • Movie ratings: One Star, Two Stars, Three Stars, Four Stars, Five Stars.

  • Temperatures in Celsius or Fahrenheit: 20째C, 30째C, 40째F, 60째F, etc.
  • IQ scores: 100, 120, 140, 160, etc. (Note: IQ scores are often treated as interval data, although the absence of a true "zero" point is a limitation.)

Ratio Scale:

  • Heights in centimeters or inches: 150 cm, 175 cm, 200 cm, etc.
  • Weights in kilograms or pounds: 50 kg, 70 kg, 90 kg, etc.
  • Income in dollars: $5000, $25000, $75000, etc.
