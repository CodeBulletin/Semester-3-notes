Role of Data analyst

A Data Analyst's role involves collecting, processing, and performing statistical analyses on data to help organizations make informed decisions. They transform raw data into actionable insights through techniques like data cleaning, analysis, and visualization. Key responsibilities include identifying data trends and patterns, preparing reports, and communicating findings to stakeholders to guide business strategies and solve problems. Data Analysts also collaborate with teams across the organization, ensuring data accuracy and creating data-driven solutions for business challenges.
