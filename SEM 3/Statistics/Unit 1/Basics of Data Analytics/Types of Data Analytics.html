Types of Data Analytics

Data analytics consists of four main types:

  1. Descriptive Analytics: Summarizes historical data to understand past events, focusing on visualizations, reports, and dashboards.
  2. Predictive Analytics: Uses historical data to build models predicting future outcomes or trends, employing machine learning, statistical modeling, and data mining.
  3. Diagnostic Analysis: Examines why specific events occurred by analyzing underlying causes and variable relationships.
  4. Prescriptive Analytics: Extends predictive analytics by suggesting actions for future outcomes, involving optimization and simulation techniques.
