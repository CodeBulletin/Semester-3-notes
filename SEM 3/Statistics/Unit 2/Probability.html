Probability

\(P(A)\) (Probability of Event A) =  \(\frac{outcomes \space that \space result \space in \space A}{Total \space Number \space of \space outcomes}\)

\(0 \le P(A) \le 1\)

\(P(A') = 1 - P(A)\)

\(P(A \cup B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A \cap B)\) 

Mutually Exclusive Events: \(P(A \cap B) = 0\) or  \(P(A \cup B) = P(A) + P(B)\)

\(P(A \cap B) = P(A | B) * P(B)\) or  \(P(A \cap B) = P(B | A) * P(A)\)

Independent Event: \(P(A | B) = P(A)\) or  \(P(A \cap B) = P(A) * P(B)\)

\(P(A) = P(Z|A_1)P(A_1)+P(Z|A_2)P(A_2) + ...\)

Compund Probability of Independent Events

\(P(AB) = P(A) * P(B)\)
