Hypergeometric Distribution

The hypergeometric distribution is a probability distribution that describes the number of successes in a fixed-size sample drawn without replacement from a finite population containing a specific number of successes and failures. It's often used in situations where the sampling is done without replacement, meaning that once an item is selected, it is not put back into the population.
