Sections Under IT ACT, 2008

Section 10A: Recognition of contracts formed through electronic means.

Section 43A: Compensation for failure to protect sensitive personal data.

Section 66: Modified to include various types of computer-related offenses.

Section 66A: Punishment for sending offensive messages through communication service (Note: This section was later struck down by the Supreme Court of India in 2015).

Section 66B: Punishment for dishonestly receiving stolen computer resource or communication device.

Section 66C: Punishment for identity theft.

Section 66D: Punishment for cheating by personation by using computer resources.

Section 66E: Punishment for violation of privacy.

Section 66F: Punishment for cyber terrorism.

Section 67: Amended to include punishment for publishing or transmitting obscene material in electronic form.

Section 67A: Punishment for publishing or transmitting material containing sexually explicit act, etc., in electronic form.

Section 67B: Punishment for publishing or transmitting material depicting children in sexually explicit act, etc., in electronic form.

Section 67C: Intermediaries required to preserve and retain specified information.

Section 69: Powers to issue directions for interception or monitoring or decryption of any information through any computer resource.

Section 69A: Power to issue directions for blocking for public access of any information through any computer resource.

Section 69B: Power to authorize to monitor and collect traffic data or information through any computer resource for cybersecurity.

Section 70: Protected systems.

Section 70A: Designation of an agency of the government to be called the Indian Computer Emergency Response Team.

Section 70B: Functions and powers of the Indian Computer Emergency Response Team.

Section 72A: Punishment for disclosure of information in breach of lawful contract.

Section 79: Exemption from liability of intermediary in certain cases, provided they observe due diligence.

Section 84B: Punishment for abetment of offenses.

Section 84C: Punishment for attempt to commit offenses.
