Unit 2

Systems of Linear Equations: Solutions of systems of Linear equations. Gauss Elimination: Direct method and pivoting. LU-decomposition method, Gauss Seidal iterative method, Gauss Jacobi iterative Method, Rate of Convergence of Gauss Seidel method, Finding inverse of a matrix using Gauss Jordan Method, Ill Conditioned system of equations, Refinement of solutions.

Interpolation and Approximation: Langrange’s Interpolation, Newton Divided difference method. Finite Differences, Polynomial Interpolation: Newton’s forward and backward formula.

Central Difference Formulae: Gauss forward and backward formula, Bessel and Stirling Method, Everett’s formula. Hermite’s Interpolation. Approximation of function by Taylor’s series and Chebyshev polynomial.
