Unit 3

Numerical Differentiation and Integration: Introduction to Numerical Differentiation. 

Numerical Integration: Trapezoidal rule, Simpson’s rules, Boole’s Rule, Weddle’s Rule, Gauss quadrature formulas, Romberg’s integration, Error in Trapezoidal Rule, Error in Simpsons' Rules.

Numerical Solution of Ordinary Differential Equations: Picard’s Method, Taylor’s series method, Euler’s Method, Modified Euler’s method. 

Runge-Kutta (RK) Methods: Second and fourth order RK methods, Automatic error monitoring, stability of solution.
