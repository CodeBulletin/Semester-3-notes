IEEE 754

IEEE 754 is a standard for floating-point arithmetic, developed by the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE). It defines the formats and rules for representing and performing arithmetic operations on floating-point numbers in computers. The IEEE 754 standard was first published in 1985 and has since been revised and extended.
