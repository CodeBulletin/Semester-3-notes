(b) State the Master theorem of recurrence to determine time complexity of an algorithm that can be solved using Divide and Conquer.

The Master Theorem is a pivotal tool in the analysis of the time complexity of divide-and-conquer algorithms. It provides a way to determine the asymptotic behavior (big O notation) of these algorithms without requiring the use of complex mathematics like recurrences or limits.
